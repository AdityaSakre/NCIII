Opportunity for Sponsors

(To interact with 2,000+ students, 200+ TPOs & 100+ Industry Professionals from Across The Country)

Dear Sir / Madam,

Greetings from Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET), Pune, Maharashtra !!

We would like to introduce ourselves as one of the reputed Educational Brand in Maharashtra with 7 institutes offering Engineering, Polytechnic, MBA, MCA, Architecture, HSC & CBSE School education.

It gives us pleasure to inform you that we are organizing 8th “National Conference on Industry Institute Interaction – 2016 (NCIII-2016)” on 28th March 2016 @Pimpri Chinchwad college of Engineering (PCCOE), Nigdi, Pune. Around 2,000+ students, 200+ Training Placement Officers (TPOs), & 100+ Industry Professionals (like Directors, Vice Presidents, Senior HR managers, HR executives, CTOs etc.) from across the country are expected to attend the conference and interact with each other to find the ways to bridge the gap between industry & academia. Our objective is to improve the employability of graduates by understanding industry expectations.

We are organizing this conference for last several years (Please refer www.NCIII.in for details). NCIII-2016 is jointly organized by NASSCOM, S. P. Pune University (SPPU), Board of Apprenticeship Training (BOAT-an autonomous body of Ministry of HRD, Government of India), & Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) in association with Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET), Pune, Maharashtra.
