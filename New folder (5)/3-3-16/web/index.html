It gives us pleasure to inform you that every year, we organize Industry Institute Interaction Meet in association with University of Pune (SPPU) & Board of Apprenticeship Training (an autonomous body of Ministry of HRD, Government of India) at PCCOE. This year MIDC (Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation) is also about to be a joint organizer. The Training Placement Officers (TPOs) of engineering institutes from across India & Directors, Vice Presidents, Senior HR managers, HR executives, CTOs & various Industry Professionals across the country attend the meet and interact with each other to find the ways to bridge the gap between industry & academia. Last year’s III meet was a grand success with participation of around 200 Training Placement Officers from 11 states & around 75 Industry professionals. This year, alongwith TPOs & Industry Professionals, around 2000 students from across the country are also expected to participate in the event.

This year, it will be 8th Industry Institute Interaction Meet at national level named “National Conference on Industry Institute Interaction (NCIII)-2016” scheduled on 28th March 2016 @Pimpri Chinchwad college of Engineering (PCCOE), Nigdi, Pune.
